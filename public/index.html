Create your own aquarium experience!
INSTRUCTIONS
  • Use the arrow keys to move your fish.
  • Add more (automated) fish to your aquarium by clicking the mouse.
  • Press the backspace key to delete a fish.
  • Press & hold 'b' (for bubble) to blow a bubble until it pops and clear away all the other fish.
  • You can also press the escape key to clear away all other fish.
  • If you start blowing a bubble and want to delete it, press 'p' (for pop).
  • Press the spacebar to start the aquarium noise. Press spacebar again to pause.
